About Us

Quality Healthcare with a Personal Touch

Sebiotimo Medical and Diagnostics Center is a trusted healthcare institution delivering comprehensive medical and diagnostic solutions for all age groups.

With a focus on preventive care, chronic disease management, and modern diagnostics, our goal is to keep you and your family healthy. From our experienced physicians to our friendly support staff, everyone here is dedicated to your well-being.
